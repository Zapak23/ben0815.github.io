Alumni

Alumni Newsletter

Xi Gamma keeps up with our brothers and sisters who have left to pursue their careers through our alumni newsletter. Each edition has information on the status of the chapter as well as updates on our growth, new pledge classes and special events.

Alumni are given the option to join our mailing list immediately after graduation. If you'd like to be added to our list, please contact us at thetatau.xigamma@gmail.com




Donate

Donations help us host many of our proffessional development and brotherhood events. Any contributions are greatly appreciated and allow us to not only host events like tailgates and community service, but also to grow as a fraternity.

For donation checks, mail them to the Texas A&M Foundation at
Address: 401 George Bush Dr, College Station, TX 77840
Phone: (979) 845-8161
In order to recieve an income tax receipt, the donors shoud make the checks out to the Texas A&M Foundation and mail the funds to the Foundation. The organization's name needs to be written in the memo line of the check.


If you have an other questions or concerns regarding donations, please don't hesitate to contact our Treasurer, Anthony Mele.
